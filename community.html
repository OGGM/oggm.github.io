Github, Open Meetings and Slack

A lot of the project communication happens on GitHub. We also have an active Slack community (reach out if you want to join in), and we organize regular, open meetings.

Mailing lists

If you want to be informed about the OGGM developments, publications or workshops please subscribe to the [OGGM-Announce] mailing list.

If you want to ask questions about the model or dicsuss with other model users please subscribe to the [OGGM-Users] mailing list.

Research projects related to OGGM


  • Predictability and Attribution of Regional Sea Level Change Caused by Glacier Mass Change (Universität Bremen)
  • Ocean-ice Interaction of peripheral Greenland Glaciers (Universität Bremen)
  • Projections of global glacier melt under low-end warming scenarios (Universität Bremen)
  • The Upper Grindelwald Glacier as indicator for Holocene climate variability (Universität Innsbruck)
  • Modelling of glacier length changes in the Alps on the base of tree-ring based temperature reconstructions for the last 2500 years (Universität Innsbruck)
  • Modelling glacier changes over the past millennium (UC Louvain)
