The OGGM team has just launched its first ever community consultation!

The survey is open until May 17, 2026, at this link:

**[OGGM community consultation 2026](https://forms.office.com/e/ZDBCwj3vFb)**

All OGGM ecosystem users (or attempted users), as well as OGGM-Edu students, are invited to take part. Whether you have used OGGM, one of the OGGM-Edu apps, worked with OGGM-Hub, or downloaded OGGM products, we’d love to hear from you.

The survey will remain open until May 17th.

Many thanks for your time! This will directly help us shape the future of OGGM.
